About FoodShare

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting food donors with those in need. Our mission is to create a sustainable solution to food waste while addressing hunger in our communities.

Every year, billions of tons of food are wasted globally while millions of people go hungry. We believe that by creating an efficient donation system, we can help bridge this gap and make a positive impact on both environmental sustainability and social welfare.

Founded as a final year project, FoodShare has grown into a community-driven initiative that brings together restaurants, grocery stores, individuals, and charitable organizations in the fight against food waste.
