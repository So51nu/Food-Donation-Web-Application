Volunteer Opportunities

We have a variety of roles to match your skills, interests, and availability. Find the perfect fit for you!

Food Sorter

Help sort and package donated food items for distribution to families in need. This role is perfect for those who enjoy hands-on work and can commit to regular shifts.

🕒 2-4 hours/week

Delivery Driver

Deliver food packages to homebound seniors and families who cannot access our distribution centers. Must have a valid driver's license and reliable vehicle.

🕒 3-5 hours/week

Event Coordinator

Help plan and execute food drives, fundraising events, and community outreach programs. Great for those with organizational and communication skills.

🕒 5-10 hours/month

Administrative Support

Assist with office tasks, data entry, phone calls, and correspondence. Perfect for those with administrative experience or those looking to develop these skills.

🕒 4-6 hours/week

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering with FoodShare.

What is the minimum age to volunteer?

The minimum age to volunteer independently is 16 years old. Youth ages 12-15 can volunteer with a parent or guardian. Children under 12 can participate in specific family-friendly events with adult supervision.

Do I need any special skills or experience?

No special skills are required for most volunteer positions. We provide all necessary training. However, some roles like delivery drivers require a valid driver's license, and administrative roles benefit from basic computer skills.

What is the time commitment?

Time commitments vary by role. Some positions require regular weekly shifts (2-4 hours), while others are more flexible or event-based. We work with your schedule to find the best fit for you.

Can I volunteer as part of a group?

Yes! We welcome corporate teams, community groups, religious organizations, and schools. Group volunteering is a great team-building activity and makes a significant impact. Contact our volunteer coordinator to arrange group opportunities.

Do you provide documentation for service hours?

Yes, we're happy to provide documentation of your volunteer hours for school, work, or court-ordered community service. Please let us know in advance if you need this documentation.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all local health guidelines and have implemented enhanced cleaning protocols, social distancing measures, and mask requirements when appropriate. The safety of our volunteers and the communities we serve is our top priority.
