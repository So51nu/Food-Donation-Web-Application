Our Journey of Impact

From our humble beginnings to our current reach, see how we've grown and expanded our impact over the years.

2015

Our Beginning

Started with a small group of volunteers distributing food packages to 50 families in one neighborhood.

2017

First Distribution Center

Opened our first permanent distribution center and expanded to serve 500 families monthly.

2019

School Program Launch

Launched our school lunch program in partnership with 10 local schools, providing meals to 2,000 children.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly scaled operations during the pandemic, serving over 20,000 families with emergency food assistance.

2022

Regional Expansion

Expanded to 35 distribution centers across the region, partnering with 100+ local organizations.

2023

Today's Impact

Currently serving 45,000+ families monthly with the help of 1,200+ dedicated volunteers.
