How One Restaurant Made a Difference

When Spice Garden restaurant in Mumbai joined our platform, they had no idea the impact they would make. What started as a simple way to reduce food waste has turned into a community-changing initiative.

Every evening, the restaurant donates unsold food that would otherwise go to waste. Through our platform, this food is quickly distributed to nearby shelters and community centers.
